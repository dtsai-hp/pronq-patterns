Teaser Images

Need an image for your blog teaser and/or blog post? We have created a library of awesome custom images for you to freely use.

Directions: Simply pick an image, right-click on that image and hit Save Image As, and then choose a file name and location and save it to your computer. To insert the image into your blog teasers and/or blog post just follow the same steps as you would for any other photo. For more help click here. (Add link to Playbook eventually)
